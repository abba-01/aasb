## The Core Hypothesis

The phrase "as above, so below" (Hermetic principle) is taken literally:
structures on the ground mirror the sky above them. This repo documents evidence
that the same sky pattern (Orion's Belt) was mirrored in ground structures on
BOTH Mars and Earth.

## The Chain of Evidence

### 1. Giza Orion Correlation (Bauval, 1989)
The three Giza pyramids (Khufu, Khafre, Menkaure) are arranged to mirror the
three stars of Orion's Belt (Alnitak, Alnilam, Mintaka) as seen from Earth at
~10,500 BCE. At that epoch, the belt barely grazed the southern horizon (altitude ~6.6°
at Giza, 30°N). The Sphinx faces due east — toward Leo at the vernal equinox dawn.

### 2. Mars Formation Match (Eric D. Martin, 2024)
The Mars Pathfinder site (19.13°N, Ares Vallis) contains:
- **North Twin Peak** — one of two prominent hills, 860m from lander
- **South Twin Peak** — second hill, ~1000m from lander
- **North Knob** — third formation (NASA-named), plateau to the right of North Twin,
  visible in IMP panoramas, confirmed in HiRISE overhead (PSP_001890_1995)
- **Sphinx-analog object** — ground-level feature between lander and formations,
  measured via T-square photogrammetry to match Great Sphinx dimensions (~66 ft)

The spatial arrangement of (North Twin + South Twin + North Knob + Sphinx-analog)
matches the spatial arrangement of (Khufu + Khafre + Menkaure + Great Sphinx)
when scaled by the IMP camera metric.

### 3. Orion Sky Comparison (2026, sky charts in images/)
From Mars at 19.13°N, Orion's Belt at 10,500 BCE reaches **17.4° altitude** at
transit — significantly higher than from Giza (6.6°). The belt was more prominently
visible from Mars at the critical epoch.

This means: if a Mars civilization aligned ground structures to Orion, they had a
better sky view to work from than Egyptian astronomers on Earth at the same epoch.

## The Sphinx Interpretation

**Standard view**: Sphinx is a lion (Leo) or a king's face on a lion body.

**Eric's interpretation**: The Sphinx face is a **Pharaoh**, specifically representing
a leader figure from Mars. The monument does not guard a temple — it guards the
*knowledge* and *history* buried within the Giza plateau (the "Hall of Records"
tradition). The Sphinx faces the direction of origin (east/rising sun = origin point).

The Sphinx may represent the **Exodus from Mars** — a monument to the civilization
that left a dying planet and rebuilt its signature monument on Earth, aligning it to
the same stars from the new home planet.

## The Sequence Hypothesis

1. Mars develops intelligent life (unknown era, before ~3.5 billion years ago or
   more recently during episodic wetter periods)
2. Mars civilization builds three pyramid-scale structures + Sphinx-analog aligned
   to Orion's Belt at 19.13°N in Ares Vallis
3. Mars becomes uninhabitable (atmosphere loss, impact events, geological change)
4. Civilization migrates — carrying astronomical knowledge including the
   Orion alignment and the scale/positional data of their monuments
5. On Earth (~10,500 BCE or ~2560 BCE), they rebuild: Giza pyramids + Sphinx,
   aligned to Orion using the same geometry, now from 30°N (Egypt)
6. The Sphinx face records the leader of the Exodus — a Pharaoh, not a deity
7. The Egyptian "gods" and kingship mythology derives from this Martian heritage —
   hence the feathered thrones (representing flight / spacecraft), divine right
   of kings, and obsession with Orion and the afterlife

## What Would Falsify This

1. **HiRISE overlay fails**: Scale the Giza layout onto PSP_001890_1995.
   If the three Mars formations do NOT match Giza positions, hypothesis is wrong.
2. **Sphinx dimensions don't match**: Recheck Eric's T-square measurements
   against independent scale calibration.
3. **North Knob is the wrong scale**: If North Knob is 10x larger or smaller
   than the Menkaure pyramid equivalent, positional match may be coincidence.

## Notes on Feathered Thrones

The Egyptian pharaoh's throne incorporated feathers (Maat feathers, Isis wings).
The Horus throne was flanked by falcon wings. Standard Egyptological interpretation:
feathers = divine protection, Maat = truth/justice.

Eric's interpretation: feathers = **flight** — the throne represents a seat of
power that came from the sky. The Pharaoh is the inheritor of those who arrived
by flight. Not metaphorical — literal.

This remains speculative and untestable with current evidence.
